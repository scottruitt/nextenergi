EnergiCube™

an integrated Microgrid for homes and businesses

The benefits and capabilities of Solar PV, Energy Storage, LEDs, and Electric Vehicles are ever increasing. At the same time, their cost curves are sharply decreasing. But deploying these technologies in isolation captures only a small fraction of their value.

What's needed is a more comprehensive and cost-effective approach: a Microgrid. EnergiCube provides the infrastructure and intelligence to rapidly deploy and monetize clean local energy Microgrids.

The economic and environmental benefits of clean local energy are many: EnergiCube loosens carbon's grip on the economy, eliminates gasoline and other fossil fuels, shifts load to avoid peak expenses, enables ancillary services to the grid and the ISO, and makes energy a locally tradable commodity.

The status quo is outdated and costly, and 19th century technology has no place in the 21st. EnergiCube is the key to unlocking our clean energy future.
